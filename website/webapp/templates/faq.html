FAQ

  • Q: How do I see my results or compare results with the majority?

    • A: Click on the Results section of the website to be able to see in depth analysis about your current state and compare it to the people who have filled the survey

  • Q: I am not sure about the privacy of the answers I have filled in the survey , Why should I fill it?

    • A: Filling the survey does not take your name or details that can pinpoint to your identity as such data is not saved with us, Only data that can help the organisation have an idea about the state of their employees for their betterment is saved and complete anonimity is provided

  • Q: I want to see the result of my Anxiety and Depression survey with the chatbot.

    • A: You can click on the result section of the website to see the kind of mental state you have right now and assess the situtation, you can also opt to inform the employer if you need any kind of break according to the result.

  • Q: The Anxiety and Depression survey seems too private to share with anyone , I dont want to share it with the organisation.

    • A: The result to that survey is for your eyes only , we only take the information from the Mental Health Tech survey not from this one.
